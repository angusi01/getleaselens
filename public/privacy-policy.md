# Privacy Policy

**LeaseLens** (getleaselens.com.au)
Last updated: 16 June 2026
Operator: AIwers Group | Birtinya, Queensland, Australia

## 1. Information We Collect

When you use LeaseLens, we may collect:

- **Uploaded documents**: Lease agreements you upload for AI analysis. These are processed and then deleted within 24 hours.
- **Email address**: If you create an account or purchase a report
- **Payment information**: Processed securely via Stripe. We do not store card details.
- **Usage data**: Pages visited, features used (via PostHog analytics)
- **Device and browser data**: IP address, browser type, operating system

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- To analyse uploaded lease documents and generate reports
- To process payments via Stripe
- To deliver reports and account-related communications via Brevo
- To improve our AI analysis service
- To comply with legal obligations

## 3. Document Handling

Lease documents uploaded for analysis are:
- Processed by our AI system to generate a plain-English report
- Stored temporarily (maximum 24 hours) then permanently deleted
- Never shared with third parties
- Never used to train AI models

## 4. Payment Processing

Payments are handled by Stripe. LeaseLens does not store credit card information. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment data: https://stripe.com/au/privacy

## 5. Cookies

We use cookies for session management and analytics (PostHog). By using this service, you consent to cookies necessary for the service to function.

## 6. Third-Party Services

- **Stripe**: Payment processing
- **PostHog**: Analytics (US Cloud)
- **Brevo**: Transactional email delivery
- **Vercel**: Hosting
- **Supabase**: Database and backend
- **Anthropic Claude API**: AI analysis of lease documents

## 7. Data Retention

- Uploaded lease documents: Deleted within 24 hours of processing
- Account data: Retained while your account is active
- Payment records: Retained for 7 years as required by Australian tax law

## 8. Your Rights (Australian Privacy Act 1988)

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights: privacy@getleaselens.com.au
